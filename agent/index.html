What is a KAT Score?

KAT (KaelAi Trust) Scores measure behavioral trustworthiness across five independent dimensions. Think of it as a credit bureau for the agent economy - but built on onchain behavior, not identity.

⏱️
TL
Transaction Longevity

How long the wallet has been active and transacting. Older wallets with consistent history score higher.

🔄
BC
Behavioral Consistency

Pattern stability over time. Erratic activity, sudden large transactions, or behavioral shifts reduce this score.

🤝
CQ
Counterparty Quality

The trustworthiness of wallets and protocols this address has interacted with. Good neighbors matter.

⚡
WA
Wallet Activity

Depth and breadth of protocol engagement. Active DeFi participants across verified protocols score higher.

📊
VS
Value Stability

Transaction value variance and patterns. Extreme spikes or anomalous value ratios signal potential risk.

Grade Scale

Grade Score Range Label Recommended Action
AAA 90-100 Exceptional trust Proceed with full commerce limits
AA 80-89 High trust - established wallet Proceed with confidence
A 70-79 Good trust - active participant Standard commerce limits apply
BBB 60-69 Moderate trust - verified active Proceed with standard limits
BB 50-59 Below average - limited history Apply reduced limits
B 40-49 Low trust - thin or erratic history Require additional verification
CCC 0-39 Very low trust - high risk signals Decline or require full KYC
