Frequently asked questions

Can I use both products on one API key?

Yes, both Agent and Shield queries are billed to the same API key. Shield queries always include the $0.05 Shield PAYG charge on top of your base tier rate. Your monthly query allowance counts toward both Agent and Shield queries.

What is a founding member rate?

Founding member pricing is locked for life while you remain subscribed. We have a limited number of founding slots. Once they're gone, new subscribers pay standard rates. Founding members who stay subscribed keep their rate indefinitely, regardless of future price increases.

Is there a free trial for Shield?

Shield mode is available on any API key as pure PAYG at $0.05/query. No subscription required to start. Use your free 100-query allowance to try Agent mode first, then top up to test Shield. There's no separate trial — Shield is available immediately on your first top-up.

What chains does Shield support?

All 10 chains supported by the KAT Score engine: ETH, SOL, BTC, TAO, Base, Polygon, BSC, Arbitrum, Avax, and Hyperliquid. Shield runs on the same underlying behavioral scoring infrastructure as Agent — both products cover all 10 chains equally.
